Indoor Plants Care Schedule

Foliage Plants

Examples: Spider Plant, Snake Plant, Peace Lily, Boston Fern

Care Instructions: Place in a sunny room for indirect, natural light. Water frequently to maintain damp soil. Keep at room temperature, avoiding extreme heat and cold.

Flowering Indoor Plants

Examples: Orchids, African Violets, Anthurium, Christmas Cactus

Care Instructions: Remove decorative sleeves, faded flowers, and yellowing leaves. Snip off dead foliage at the base of the leaf stem.

Succulents and Cactus

Examples: Aloe Vera, Jade Plant, Echeveria, Christmas Cactus

Care Instructions: Water at least once a week with a good soak, allowing excess water to drain. Allow the compost to dry slightly between waterings.

Herbs (Indoor)

Examples: Basil, Mint, Rosemary, Parsley

Care Instructions: Most herbs need six hours of direct sunlight and a temperature of 65 to 70°F day and 55 to 60°F night. Balance humidity with adequate air circulation.
