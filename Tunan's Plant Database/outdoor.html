Outdoor Plants Care Schedule

Flowering Outdoor Plants

Examples: Roses, Tulips, Daisies, Sunflowers

Care Schedule: Water more frequently in high temperatures, low humidity, windy conditions, or as plants grow larger. Water less during cooler temperatures or overcast days.

Shrubs and Bushes

Examples: Azaleas, Boxwood, Hydrangeas, Forsythia

Care Schedule: Plant at the right time, schedule watering, follow proper feeding habits, and prune at the right times.

Trees

Examples: Oak, Maple, Pine, Cherry Blossom

Care Schedule: A newly planted tree should be watered every three to five days during the growing season, and once a month during dry periods in the dormant season.

Vegetables (Outdoor)

Examples: Tomatoes, Bell Peppers, Carrots, Lettuce

Care Schedule: Provide adequate soil moisture throughout the growing season for strong roots and fruitful production.
